Lemon Gaba SDA Choir Projects

The Lemon Gaba SDA Choir is dedicated to spreading the message of faith and hope through music. Our projects range from creating inspiring music to engaging with the community, all aimed at glorifying God and uplifting others.

Music Production & Album Releases

  • Project Overview: The choir is committed to producing high-quality gospel music that reflects the values and beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. We release albums that resonate with both local and global audiences, incorporating traditional hymns and contemporary gospel. Each album project involves collaboration among choir members, music producers, and guest artists, resulting in a diverse collection of uplifting songs.
  • Current Project: We are in the process of recording our next album, which will feature both original compositions and traditional African gospel hymns.

Community Outreach

Project Overview: The choir is actively involved in outreach programs that aim to bring hope and joy to underprivileged communities. Through performances, charity concerts, and music workshops, we seek to make a positive impact on the lives of others, especially in remote areas where access to spiritual and musical enrichment may be limited. Current Project: Our next community outreach project will take us to rural areas where we will host free music workshops for local youth and perform for the community to spread encouragement and faith.
